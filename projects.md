## Numerical Solution of the Time-Independent Schrödinger Equation (TISE)

🔗 **Project Repository:**
[Animation_1D_SE_Morse](https://github.com/elvira123-hub/Animation_1D_SE_Morse)

This project presents computational tools and numerical methods commonly used in **quantum mechanics**, **materials science**, and **quantum chemistry**. It provides a simple and intuitive framework for numerically solving the **time-independent Schrödinger equation (TISE)** in one, two, and three dimensions using finite-difference methods and Kronecker products.

📄 **Relevant publication:**
[**Strong Cu(I)–He interaction at open metal sites enables isotope-selective helium adsorption**](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41467-026-70901-6)
