## Selected Publications

### Direct evidence for ligand-enhanced activity of Cu(I) sites

**E. G. Dongmo**, S. Haque, F. Kreuter, J. Jin, T. Wulf, R. Tonner-Zech, T. Heine, K. R. Asmis  
*Chemical Science*, 2024, 15, 14635–14643.  

**DOI:** [10.1039/D4SC04582C](https://doi.org/10.1039/D4SC04582C)

**Summary:** Experimental and computational investigation demonstrating how ligand coordination enhances the activity of Cu(I) sites and improves hydrogen isotope separation through stronger dihydrogen adsorption.
